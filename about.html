Our Story

Founded in Nairobi, Astra Consultants was born from a vision to empower Kenyan entrepreneurs. We saw the hurdles businesses face—complex registrations, weak branding, and limited digital presence—and set out to simplify these challenges.

With expertise in company registration, graphic design, web development, and digital marketing, our team delivers tailored solutions that drive growth. We’re proud to have helped over 500 businesses across Kenya establish and thrive in competitive markets.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding Kenyan businesses to success with innovative and reliable solutions.

Mission

To deliver affordable, efficient, and comprehensive services that simplify business setup, enhance branding, and amplify digital presence for Kenyan entrepreneurs.

Vision

To be Kenya’s top consultancy, transforming businesses into market leaders through creativity, expertise, and exceptional service.
